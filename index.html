code x lit projects
Hey! I'm glad you're here. I made these mini projects at Hack@Brown and a literary arts class I took that are a mix of programmatic literary pieces!
coloringbooks
data visualization project built with Zach Baytosh, Star Su, and Bethany Hung that explores how literature can be translated into colors. (special thanks to word2vec)
type
my poem generator that uses markov chains to show the preservation of literary voice, emphasized by different type. (shout out to datamuse and rita)
indulge
a poem about decadence authored by bonappetit, dorian gray, some neural nets, and me
tempus
a fridge magnet generator where you can revise Walt Whitman's Leaves of Grass and see your revisions of each text by temperature. (thanks again, rita, and the jquery library!)
lightweight
bite-sized data visualization of the lightness of text through a zine. (bless numpy for letting me do that number witchcraft and d3 for automating my visualizations)
